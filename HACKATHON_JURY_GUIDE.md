### 1. The "Hook": Why QuMail? (The Problem)
**Imagine this:** You have a digital safe where you keep your most private letters. You use a very complex lock that would take today's fastest supercomputers **billions of years** to pick. You feel safe, right?

**The Reality Check:** A new kind of computer is comingâ€”the **Quantum Computer**. It doesn't work like your laptop; it uses the laws of physics to solve math problems that were previously "unsolvable." What took billions of years will soon take **seconds**.

**The "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later" (HNDL) Threat:** 
Adversaries are already stealing encrypted emails *today*, even if they can't read them yet. They are waiting for **Q-Day** (the day a cryptographically relevant quantum computer is born) to unlock everything they've ever stolen. **Standard encryption (Gmail, Outlook) is effectively a "time-delayed" open book against this threat.**

---

### 2. Our Solution: QuMail
QuMail is a first-of-its-kind secure email platform that combines:
1.  **PQC (Post-Quantum Cryptography):** Mathematical algorithms that quantum computers can't solve.
2.  **QKD (Quantum Key Distribution) Simulation:** Physics-based key sharing that detects eavesdropping.
3.  **Zero-Knowledge Architecture:** We ensure **Zero Plaintext at Rest**. Even if someone steals our database, they get nothing but "noise."

### 3. The Tech Stack (Our Arsenal)
*   **Frontend:** React 18 + Vite + Tailwind CSS + Framer Motion (Premium, futuristic UI/UX).
*   **Backend:** Node.js, Express, TypeScript.
*   **Cryptography:** 
    *   **NIST Winner:** Implementation of **CRYSTALS-Kyber** (Level 3).
    *   **ETSI Standard:** Key Management Entity (KME) following **ETSI GS QKD-014** REST API.
*   **Persistence:** PostgreSQL (Metadata) & MongoDB (Secure Payload Storage) with **Drizzle ORM**.
